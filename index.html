Tired of watching hours of video material and online blogs about making ( quick ) online money for free?! Well, i am. And this is exactly the reason why i launched this website.

Not so much reality.

If you're looking to get rich or wealthy overnight you are in the wrong place. Not just this one I'm afraid but nearly everywhere else on the internet too. Well, that said it's not impossible either. You can always purchase a good thriving website on flippa.com and either sell it or make money from it, wait for it... Overnight! Yes, if you're lucky... But if you are like me at the beginning of my journey or as the majority of people searching "How to make money online" I bet financial resources are not humongous. So the freebie side of making money online takes time. It certainly doesn't come overnight. (There is a but) BUT, if you are willing to invest a certain amount of you're time and stick with your projects (don't hop to a different one each other day) there is hope!

Reality.

Luckily for us, the reality is not very complicated. As mentioned in the more depressive card on the left, it takes time, a little bit of a learning curve (depends on what you're getting in to) and some common sense. Look at making money online the same way as growing a plant or a tree. You always start with planting the seed. Depending on the route you are taking or what niche you are going for this will be most likely getting your actual website, blog or any other project online. Now, the seed has sprouted and needs nutrients, water, love, and attention. You can compare this stage with feeding your beloved project some articles, or how about learning SEO (search engine optimization) to get your site ranked high in the search results! And of course, we all need traffic... Then, after all of this, we finally get to the blooming stage. Where all of our hard work results in a beautiful moneymaking tree! Ok, enough about the story. You can see where I'm going with this. Be patient you will be rewarded, that's reality.
